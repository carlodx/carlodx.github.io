BIO

I am a Paris-based sound engineer and former software engineer, working across production, recording, mixing, mastering, and live sound. I graduated major of my class at Abbey Road Institute Paris, where I built a strong foundation in both the technical and creative sides of music production.

Before moving into audio, I worked in software engineering and research — an experience that shaped my precision, curiosity, and problem-solving mindset. Today, I love combining that analytical background with a deep passion for sound and collaboration.

I’m an affable, communicative person who thrives in environments where technology and art intersect — whether in the studio, on stage, or in experimental projects that push the boundaries of how we experience sound.
